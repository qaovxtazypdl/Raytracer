  1. Additional Primitives (Cone, Hyperboloid, Torus, Cylinder)
  2. Constructive Solid Geometry
  3. Adaptive Anti-Aliasing
  4. Soft Shadows
  5. Glossy Reflection/Refraction
  6. Texture Mapping
  7. Bump Mapping
  8. Refraction
  9. Anaglyph 3D
  10. Final Scene
  11. Threading
  12. Generalized Bounding Boxes
And in A4, Mirror Reflection.
